In subscribing to this philosophy, The User acknowledges that optimism is a legitimate response to uncertainty, that weather models are probabilistic in nature, that any single model may be correct on any given day, and that it is at least theoretically possible that The App will be right.

Kasper and Jan do not warrant that The App will be right. They hope it will be right. Hope is not a warranty.
